## Delete Your Account

You may request deletion of your U-Own account at any time.

---

## 1. How to Request Deletion

You can delete your account directly from within the **U-Own** app:

    1. Open the "More" page (In your main app navigation bar).
    2. Tap "Edit Your Account".
    3. Confirm your choice to permanently delete your account.

If you are unable to access the app, you may also contact us at:

**contact@vprojectz.com**

Please include the email address linked to your account so we can verify ownership.

---

## 2. What Happens When Your Account Is Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data will be **permanently removed**:

- Your account information (email, profile, and related settings).
- Any app-specific preferences stored on our servers.

The following data may be **retained for a limited time** where required by law or for security purposes:

- Transaction records (retained for up to 30 days).
- Error logs and usage data (retained anonymously for system integrity).

---

## 3. Can I Restore My Account?

Once deleted, your account **cannot be restored**. You will need to create a new account to use U-Own again.

---
